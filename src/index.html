Children's Program - CP

Children's Program (CP) is specifically designed for children from 8 to 10 years of age. The program is conducted part-time. The full duration of the program is 8 terms (2 years).

Young Learners' Program - YLP

Young Learners Program (YL) is specifically designed for adolescent students from 11 to 14 years of age. The program is conducted part-time.

General English Program - GEP

General English Program is a part-time program designed for students wishing to achieve an upper-intermediate level of English. For students who start at the beginner level.

Diploma in English

Diploma in English is designed for all students, current and aspiring professionals, business executives and those who want to perfect their English through a wide variety of challenging and interesting subject matter and materials.

IELTS Preparation Course

The course covers the essentials of what candidates need to know about the Academic IELTS test, including test administration, the system of band scores (1-9) and the advantages of IELTS for study, work and migration.

Customized Training Service

ACE’s customised programs are designed in consultation with clients to ensure their specific training needs are met. Placement testing, development of specific curricula and materials, delivery of training, evaluation and reporting.

Customized Testing Program

ACE has been meeting the English language testing needs of Cambodian government ministries, donor organisations, NGOs and corporations in Cambodia since 1992
